Do you see someone making a logical fallacy on Reddit? Or are you just looking to learn more about logical fallacies?

Welcome to Fallacy.in

This website contains a list of logical fallacies (with an explanation and example) and serves as data host and front-end companion for Reddit bot.

How to use reddit bot?

Right now, the bot is in testing stage. You can test it out at /r/fallacybottest by making a comment "keyword + fallacy name", eg "fallacy in appeal to probability".

Keywords are "what is", "what's", "explain" or "fallacy in". Lists of formal and informal fallacies are at the main page. To add missing ones, check Contribute page.
