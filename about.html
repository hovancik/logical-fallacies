Hello!

Do you see someone making a logical fallacy on reddit? Or are you just looking to learn about logical fallacies?

Welcome to Fallacy.in

This website contains a list of logical fallacies (with an explanation and example) and serves as data host and front-end companion for Reddit bot.

How to use reddit bot?

Right now, the bot is in testing. You can test it out at /r/fallacybottest by making a comment keyword fallacy name, eg fallacy in appeal to probability.

Keywords are what is, what's, explain or fallacy in.

Fallacies can be:
appeal to the stone
conjunction fallacy
base rate fallacy
argument from fallacy
appeal to probability
