Circular cause and consequence

Circular cause and consequence is an informal fallacy when the consequence of the phenomenon is said to be an unavoidable cause of the phenomenon when the truth may be otherwise.

My music CD is not popular because people are not listening to it. And people are not listening to it because it is not popular. If they would only listen to it, it would become popular.
